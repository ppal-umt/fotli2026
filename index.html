About Us

FoTLI 2026 is an online competition challenging educators and students to create impactful solutions for the future of education.

Our Objectives

  • Increase awareness and involvement in exploring improvements to teaching and learning.
  • Enhance creativity and innovation through the creation of new, beneficial solutions.
  • Provide formal recognition and appreciation to those who contribute creative ideas.

Rules & Regulations

Please read carefully to avoid disqualification.

Participation

Max 5 Members/Group

General Rule: For group entries, the identity and role of all participating members must be clearly stated and labeled.

Category A
  • Primary School Students
  • Secondary School Students

Note: For this category, teachers may only participate as Project Advisors, providing guidance and support to the student team. They are NOT eligible to compete as participants.

Category B
  • Matriculation / Polytechnic Students
  • Public & Private University Students

Note: For this category, lecturers may only participate as Project Advisors, providing guidance and support to the student team. They are NOT eligible to compete as participants.

Category C
  • Teachers/Lecturers from Matriculation / Polytechnic
  • Lecturers from Public & Private Universities

Note: For this category, teachers/lecturers may participate as team members alongside students.

Video

  • • 5-7 Minutes Duration.
  • • Submit via YouTube Link.
  • • Malay or English Only.

Innovation Description

  • • Problem: Define issue & affected users.
  • • Solution: State innovation & core concept.
  • • Mechanics: Outline step-by-step & technology.
  • • Impact: Highlight tangible benefits & impacts.
  • • Status: Detail current stage & next steps.

Policy

  • • No Information Changes Allowed After Submission.
  • • No Refunds For Mistakes.
